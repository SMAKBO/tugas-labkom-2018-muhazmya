TOXICITY PHENOL

Dangers of Phenol For human health:

  • Corrosive to eyes, skin and respiratory tract.
  • If inhaled can cause edema in the lungs.
  • May affect the central nervous system, liver and kidney, causing coma, stomach damage, respiratory failure, and death.
  • Contact with the skin will cause dermatitis.

    • Dangers of Phenol For Fish :

  • Dangerous for fish at levels of 10 to 100 mg.

  • Phenol will affect the metabolism, growth, and reproductive ability of fish.
