PHYSICAL PROPERTIES OF PHENOL

  1. Phenol pure colorless crystals
  2. Very odorous and has antiseptic properties
  3. Somewhat soluble in water and otherwise a little water can also dissolve in liquid phenol
  4. Since the molecular weight of the water is low and the molal freezing point of the phenol is high, is 7.5, a mixture of phenol with 5-6% water has formed liquid at regular temperature. The phenol solution in water is called carbolic or carbolic acid.
